Melbourne's streets, malls, and laneways are home to a quietly growing collection of public pianos — instruments waiting to be played, discovered, and shared. This site exists to spread the love and joy of piano music, and to be part of the journey of every musician who sits down at one of these keys — whether it's your first nervous melody or a confident concert performance.

Find a piano near you. Share one you've discovered. Keep the music alive.

Forest Hill Chase

Forest Hill Chase

Alex Steinbach · Mini Grand

A white Alex Steinbach mini grand tucked inside this quieter suburban shopping centre. Generally in reasonable condition with relatively low use, meaning it tends to hold its tune well. The signage officially states it's to be played when booked, but play nicely and respectfully and the community tends to enjoy it.

🏬 Shopping Centre 📍 Forest Hill, VIC

Melbourne Central

Melbourne Central

Yamaha · Baby Grand

One of Melbourne's most iconic public pianos, this Yamaha baby grand sees serious daily use and attracts some genuinely impressive players. It's regularly maintained, but the piano is moved every night when the centre closes — so tuning can be hit or miss. Catch it on a good day and it sings. A real community centrepiece.

🎹 Player: Unknown

🏬 Shopping Centre 📍 Melbourne CBD
Palace Cinema Como

Palace Cinema Como

Kawai · Baby Grand

Nestled in the platinum lounge of the beautifully preserved Palace Cinema Como on Toorak Road, this Kawai baby grand is a gem. It has a lovely touch and warm, resonant tone that suits the grand art-deco surrounds. A more intimate and quiet setting — perfect for an unhurried session before a film.

🎹 Callum Wolfenden · Scott Joplin – Maple Leaf Rag

🎦 Cinema Lounge 📍 South Yarra

Westfield Doncaster

Westfield Doncaster

Yamaha · Baby Grand

Found at the base of the main staircase near David Jones, this Yamaha baby grand is presented on a proper mini stage with rope bollards — it means business. Generally good sound and well looked-after. The signage requests experienced pianists only, so come prepared. A high-traffic, somewhat visible spot — but it's a lovely instrument for those who are ready for it.

🎹 TikTok influencer musical maestro

🏬 Shopping Centre 📍 Doncaster, VIC
Eden Oak, Geelong

Eden Oak, Geelong

Baby Grand · Geelong

A beautiful piano at Eden Oak in Geelong — a little further afield for Melbourne players but well worth the trip. A stunning instrument in an equally stunning setting.

🎹 Callum Wolfenden · Chopin – Nocturne in C♯ minor, Lento con gran espressione

📍 Geelong, VIC
